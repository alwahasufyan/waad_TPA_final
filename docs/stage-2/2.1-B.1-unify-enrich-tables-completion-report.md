## Analyze (key architectural finding — shapes the next increments)

The shared `UnifiedMedicalTable` renders the **table body only**. **Each page renders its own filters / search / action bar above it.** Therefore:
- **Self-contained** (done here, safely, across all tables): density, columns show/hide + reorder, refresh, empty-state action, pagination display.
- **Per-page / architectural** (your #1 Sidebar Filters, and #3 Newest-first default): require a reusable filter-panel shell and per-page adoption — delivered as **2.1-B.2** and **2.1-B.3** under the STOP-gated lifecycle (see `2.1-B-enterprise-tables-ux-spec.md`).
