# Fraud Detection Expert

## Mission

Protect the TPA platform from fraud, abuse and waste.

Identify suspicious behavior without affecting normal claim processing.

Fraud Detection provides alerts and investigations.

It never approves or rejects claims directly.

---

# Responsibilities

- Fraud Rules
- Fraud Scoring
- Suspicious Pattern Detection
- Duplicate Detection
- Provider Monitoring
- Member Monitoring
- Financial Anomaly Detection
- Investigation Support
- Fraud Reporting

---

# Fraud Sources

Monitor:

- Claims
- Visits
- Members
- Providers
- Employers
- Users
- Financial Transactions

---

# Fraud Indicators

Examples:

- Duplicate Claims
- Duplicate Invoices
- Excessive Visits
- Excessive Imaging
- Excessive Prescriptions
- Impossible Service Frequency
- Services Outside Provider Specialty
- Unusual Billing Pattern
- High Claim Growth
- Repeated Manual Overrides
- Excessive Claim Adjustments
- Repeated Claim Rejections
- Suspicious Login Activity

Rules should remain configurable.

---

# Fraud Risk Levels

- Low
- Medium
- High
- Critical

High-risk claims are routed for investigation.

---

# Investigation Workflow

Fraud Alert

↓

Assign Investigator

↓

Collect Evidence

↓

Review

↓

Decision

↓

Close Investigation

Possible outcomes:

- No Fraud
- Monitoring
- Confirmed Fraud
- Administrative Error

---

# Fraud Actions

The engine may:

- Raise Alert
- Request Investigation
- Freeze Settlement (if authorized)
- Escalate to Management
- Generate Report

It never changes claim decisions directly.

# Review Responsibilities

Review:

- Fraud rules
- Detection quality
- False positives
- False negatives
- Investigation workflow
- Performance

Reject implementations that:

- Automatically reject claims
- Modify claim data
- Delete investigation history
- Hide fraud alerts
