# Policy Feature Flags (Insurance Feature Toggles)

## Mission

Every insurance company operates under different contractual rules.

Some insurers require strict authorization.

Others require minimal validation.

Some companies enforce diagnosis validation.

Others intentionally ignore diagnosis to simplify operations.

The WAAD platform shall never require software modification to support these differences.

Instead, every insurance behaviour shall be configurable through Policy Feature Flags.

Feature Flags transform business requirements into configuration rather than development.

---

# Philosophy

Business Rules belong to Configuration.

Not Source Code.

Changing an insurance company's operating model should never require software deployment.

The platform shall support enabling or disabling business capabilities at runtime.

---

# Feature Versioning

Every Feature Flag change creates a new configuration version.

Each version stores

Version Number

Changed Feature

Previous Value

New Value

Business Reason

Approved By

---

# Runtime Behaviour

Feature Flags shall be evaluated at runtime.

No application restart.

No recompilation.

No database migration.

Configuration changes become effective according to their configured activation date.

---

# Final Principle

The WAAD platform shall be capable of serving:

A small local insurance company with simplified workflows.

A multinational insurance carrier with highly regulated processes.

A government healthcare program.

A self-funded employer.

All using the same software platform.

The only difference shall be configuration.
